Emily Lagattolla, Mentor

Emily Lagattolla is a veteran teacher from Chicago who has also taught in Mexico and Panama. She has special experience mentoring Colombian high school students and other Spanish speakers on their journies to higher education in the United States. Emily graduated from the University of Chicago in 2013 with a degree in English Literature. She went on to study Web Development at Lambda School in 2019, and furthered her career in the continuing education field of coding bootcamps, working as a Career Advisor to adult web development students at DigitalCrafts before founding Wildcard Mentorship in 2020.
